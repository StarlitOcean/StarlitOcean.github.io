Fire Water Steam

  1. Chapter

    2. Velvette was in Wallace’s gym. She was in her usual frilly, sky blue dress. Her nails were a bright red and she was wearing a navy blue belt on which were 3 pokeballs. Her black hair were held back in a high pony tail. She soon adjusted her glasses as she saw a man in his early 30s enter. He had black hair and narrow eyes, which made her look in his eyes. He looked back.


    The young Kalosian, felt a thunderwave. It was love at first sight. She rarely felt that, but the determination in his eyes was piercing her very soul.


    The handsome young man didn’t notice her though, as he was looking around for a challenger. However, he decided to take on Velvette first.


    “Hey, I’m Kabu Takeda. Are you new here ?” asked Kabu curiously.


    “Why yes, have you come before ?” asked Velvette curiously with a grin.


    “Well, doesn’t my age show that ?” chuckled Kabu.


    “Well,...yeah...probably...sorry. I wish you success. If you win, do you plan on taking on the Elite Four ? That would be interesting !” said Velvette stabilizing her chin with her one hand.


    “Yes. This time I will defeat Jurgen.” said Kabu with a smirk.


    “You must have tried plenty of times.” said Velvette saddened. Just then determination lit Kabu’s eyes. “Well, don’t think I will go easy on you. I will pour all over you like a waterfall.” said Velvette her challenger greeting tagline.


    “Well, I will be everburning like a volcano !” said Kabu with a smirk.


    “Well, we are gonna end up in a tie. Steam.” thought Velvette and blushed.


    “Did I say something ?” asked Kabu as he noticed her blushing.


    “Well, water plus fire equals steam. Let’s see who wins.” she smirked and got out her Sealeo. Kabu smirked too and got out his Camerupt, he also had a Torkoal, a Ninetails, a Blaziken, and a Magcargo.


    Kabu lost to Velvette one-shooting his pokemon down. Kabu pinched his nose for his defeat.


    “Hey, don’t fell bad. At least...we end up in somewhat of a steam. C’mon, let’s get out and have a coffee in one of the traditional coffee shops of Sootopolis City.” suggested Velvette and gave him her hand in the name of good sportsmanship.


    “Thank you. You are nice.” said Kabu and they both got out with a smile.


    Getting to a coffee shop. Kabu and Velvette sat by a wooden table with two chairs. They ordered coffee and Velvette looked deep in his black eyes. Kabu got the message from her eyes’ intense infatuated look that she liked him. Thinking that she was beautiful, kind and he was old enough while having lost plenty of chances to get married.


    “I like you.” he said with a smirk. Velvette blushed, dropped her jaw and hastily closed it to adjust her glasses. “You said it yourself, fire and water end up in steam. By the way; our battle was so intense I forgot your name !” said Kabu with a smirk.


    “O...oh...oh ! Haha ! My name is Velvette Dubois.” she said and looked lovingly in his eyes yet again. She didn’t want to lose sight of them.


    “That sounds Kalosian.” said Kabu and rubbed his chin.


    “It is.” she said joyfully.


    “Well, um...I want to tell you that...I also like you !” said Velvette blushing and gave him a sweet yet sultry look, unintentionally.


    They sat in the coffee shop for sometime and exchanged PokeNav codes to call one another. In the end of the day Velvette looked at the PokeNav code under the name Kabu and thought of the new man in her life, first too. When she was close to sleep she turned it off and fell asleep with Kabu in her mind.


    In Kabu’s home in Fallarbor Town. He was in his room marking his calendar for the next date with Velvette while he was at his toolshed writing a small poem on a wooden heart with a pyrography pen. Later tired, went to sleep and dreamed of Velvette and their next date and him giving her the present he made her. That little poem on the wooden heart.

  2. Chapter

    3. The next date was given by Velvette to go visit Slateport City, which she loved. The modern roads combined with the shore ahead. Was the best setting for both Velvette and Kabu from all the conversations they had over the phone.


    “I really hope he will like this restaurant. Rouge adores it.” thought Velvette nervously. It was a fact. Rouge was visiting Velvette often in Hoenn and they had dinner there. However, Velvette was living in Sootopolis City, it wasn’t that far from it on a Tropius she had for commuting.


    Soon enough Kabu appeared with a pure smile and a bouquet of orange roses.


    “These are for you, Velvette.” he said with a blush that wasn’t visible in the darkness of the night sky. The lights of Slateport were stopping before the beach and that restaurant was only serving Sushi.


    Kabu and Velvette entered and took their seats around a table. Soon a waiter approached them and asked them what they’d have. Kabu loving spicy food since always, he ordered some sushi with tamato berry and Velvette asked for some with oran berry.

I...I’m giving it to you now because it’s the end of a wonderful night with a wonderful woman.” he said and looked at her with his most charming smirk. Velvette blushed and let a small “o” escape her mouth. “You are so cute ! Anyhow, let me read this poem to you...or would you like to read it alone ?” asked Kabu his smirk turning into an innocent, but sweet smile.

    “Velvette, a name so meaningful.

    Silky skin and lips, ever so wonderful.

    Brown eyes made of precious velvet.

    I can’t resist, my heart ablaze.

    Fire to Water, I am amazed.”


    Kabu finished his poem. Velvette removed her round-rimmed glasses and wiped the tears off her eyes.


    “That’s...that’s...that’s so beautiful !” she said and fell in his arm to cry in his chest.


    “Aww, please Velvette don’t cry. I didn’t write it for you to cry.” said Kabu, but soon he noticed that she was silent.


    “Shh !” she said. She was getting lulled by his heartbeat. “That poem gave me goosebumps. Simply because all my previous loves were one-sided, which saddens me, but now I found you...and now the poem, the sound of the waves crushing on the shore, the crescent moon, the orange roses and most of all your presence. Listening to you talking and your heartbeat. I’m being so emotionally charged...I want to cry. This can’t be real. Sorry for being clingy.” Velvette felt a lump in her throat.


    “You aren’t clingy. I always looked for someone who would love someone proclaimed a loser so many times and still having the courage to challenge the Elite Four and be called loser yet again.” he chuckled. He continued. “Many left me because of me failing the Elite Four repeatedly, getting mocked by everyone in my Town and then retry only to be called a loser once more. It hurts to be with someone just because they think they are with someone strong because they see you train with your pokemon all day and believe they will be the fiance of the Champion of Hoenn.” said Kabu. Thinking his state in life made him feel sad. Velvette looked up to him.


    “I love you for being, who you are.” she said with a grin.


    “And who am I ?” asked Kabu with a grim look.


    “You are a man with determination and skills, a beautiful smile and an unbeatable spirit. You don’t know the words “give up” and I admire you for that, Kabu !” she said looking deep into his black eyes, which were shining under the silver moon like beads of obsidian. Her chocolate brown eyes, were taking in all his handsome features, making sure they would stay registered in her brain for all night. She wanted to dream of him.


    “You know, Velvette, I never thought I’d get to date a girl like you. So emotional, pure, understanding and...You are a gift from Arceus himself !” said Kabu holding her hands while smiling at her. An angelic smile.


    “Just a warning though; if you ever break my heart…I will make sure you will regret it !” she said and fell back in his chest. He hugged her tightly.


    “Don’t you worry, I believe we are made for one another !” with that they spent some more time together walking along the shore. Velvette was holding her heels and Kabu was telling her various things and it would still be fascinating. Whether it was about the weather, pokemon, the Elite Four or his plans.


    After that they left each towards his home; Kabu to Fallarbor Town, where he fell asleep as soon as he got there and Velvette at Sootopolis City where she hanged the Wooden heart with the poem, then she placed the orange roses in a vase and finally she fell asleep tiredly thinking of the man of her heart. This was the last thought of her day: “I wish the next time he gets to kiss me. I really want to taste his lips ! Oh, dear Arceus, I have no strength left to write a thing in my diary !” With her make up on she slept in a matter of seconds with the aroma of the roses filling the small apartment.

  3. Chapter

    4. Kabu was at the gym he had adapted in one of the rooms of his house. He was laying on a special for the exercise counter and lifting weights. Soon he was done and went to the wooden part of the floor to start doing push-ups. Soon though his PokeNav started ringing. He let a deep breath and got up. With a red towel, he wiped off the sweat off his forehead and his toned body. He poured some water from his bottle over his wet from sweat, black hair and went ahead to pick up the PokeNav. It read “Velvette”. He grinned and picked it.


    “Hey baby.” he said joyfully.


    “Ba...baby ?” stuttered Velvette. She finally was his official baby. “Hi, em...how are you ?” she asked curling a lock of her jet black hair.

She imagined him at the beach playing racket with her and his toned torso being exposed. “Velvette you there ?” asked Kabu again.

    “I just...I have doubts, but I don’t want to be a buzzkill.” he said with a tired look.

    “Of course. You are so sweet, thoughtful, elegant, classy, understanding. I love you with all my heart. See you tonight at Rustboro City. Kisses.” Velvette greeted him and went towards her bedroom to start jumping on her bed with her Azumarill hugged tightly in her arms.

Working as an Ace Trainer in Wallace’s gym, a water type gym, required blue clothes, so all her dresses were blue. One had a v-neck one in navy blue with silver, her frilly one, a low cut one, a plunging neckline one, a strapless one for summer, one with rhinestones and a finally a backless. She decided to go with the strapless despite being spring and therefore a bit cold. The rest were to suggestive.

    “Won’t you have some ?” she asked.

    “You are precious to me, Kabu.” she said with sweetness and tender love.

The Quarter moon was shining in her eyes and then Kabu’s hot breath fell on her wanting lips. He kissed her deeply. Every moment was so precious she kept it as a keepsake until now in Galar where they are married for years. However, the story is only starting here...

  4. Chapter

Kabu smirked over the phone and told her some sweet nothings like; “You’re the princess of my heart.” or “In your eyes the stars are shining.” eventually they both hung up.

  5. Chapter
